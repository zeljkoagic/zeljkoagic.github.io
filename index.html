Hi! I'm an assistant professor in computer science at the IT University of Copenhagen.

I do research in natural language processing (NLP).

I focus on syntactic and semantic parsing of natural language, with particular interest in cross-lingual learning, and in NLP for under-resourced languages.

Short Bio

  • postdoctoral researcher (2015--2016) in Copenhagen with Anders Søgaard in project LOWLANDS
  • postdoctoral researcher (2014) in Potsdam with Alexander Koller
  • postdoctoral researcher (2012--2013) and junior researcher (2006-2012) in Zagreb

  • projects: LOWLANDS, PARSEME, XLike, CESAR, LetsMT, ACCURAT, CLARIN, RMJT
  • LinkedIn, Google Scholar

  • PhD (2012) in Information Science from the University of Zagreb, Croatia (FFZG)
  • MSc (2005) in Computer Science from the University of Split, Croatia (FESB)
