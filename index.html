Hey there!

I work as senior data scientist at Unity in Copenhagen, Denmark.

Prior to joining Unity, I was head of applied science at Corti in Copenhagen. At Corti, we built intelligent machines for augmented medicine. Notably we helped medical personnel make crucial decisions in cases of medical emergency. Corti's AI works in real time, and it actually saves lives.

Before Corti, I spent several years in academia, where I worked at six universities and held many (or almost all?) academic positions, from PhD student to associate professor with tenure.

I researched into applied machine learning, specifically natural language processing (NLP), a field of artificial intelligence where we create machines capable of understanding human languages. I focused mostly on syntactic and semantic parsing of natural language, with particular interest in cross-lingual learning, and in processing low-resource languages.

I still maintain a tie to academic research as an (external) assistant professor at the University of Split in Croatia, which is also very close to my hometown.

Do drop me a line if you're interested in my work.
