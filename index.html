Hey!

I'm an assistant professor in computer science at the IT University of Copenhagen.

My research is in natural language processing (NLP), a field of artificial intelligence where we create machines capable of understanding human languages.

I focus on syntactic and semantic parsing of natural language,
with particular interest in cross-lingual learning, and in NLP for low-resource languages.

Do drop me a line if you're interested in my work.
